My Story

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed storytelling and have been curious about why things happen the way they do. Questioned why do people behave a certain way, and why one chooses to make a certain choice over the other. This curiosity led me to wonder and find answers to a lot of “Whys”.

So that’s how I look at data. I believe each piece of data has a story to tell and a “why” to answer. These answers have led me to have a better understanding of the pain points and come up with creative solutions to add value to businesses and people around me.

Before I started as an Analyst, I have worked for more than 4 years in India in various marketing roles in industries such as Technology, Pharmaceutical, Manufacturing, Media and Entertainment.

I want to work with teams and companies wanting to create a difference in this world and make it a little better each day. The dream is ‘to put a dent in the Universe’ - Noah Wyle.
