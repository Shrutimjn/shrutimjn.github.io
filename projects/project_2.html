Email Marketing Plan

Event Marketing Plan

  • Used the past registrations data to optimize emails, social media content, website, brochures and in-person marketing campaigns
  • Ran social media campaigns for all events through posts on Instagram(text posts, reels, stories), Linkedin (posts, speaker series)

Social Media Marketing Plan

  • Designed a monthly content calendar in advance based on upcoming events, important days and audience insights
  • Managed a team of 3 members by delegating tasks and coordinating with internal and external team
  • Generated reports of weekly progress of each platform and audience insights and made changes to strategy accordingly
