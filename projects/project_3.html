Database for Foxcore Retail

Building Relational Database for Foxcore Retail

Built a database for Foxcore - Event Merchandise Company using Teradata

Problem Statement

  • Foxcore would record sales and transaction details with pen and paper, moved them to excel spreadsheets to calculate commissions and keep track of product inventory in relation to the events
  • Information was stored in different spreadsheets and it became increasingly difficult to maintain this with the new business operations
  • This resulted in a major administrative hassle, data inconsistency, and delay in retrieving data for strategic insights and decision-making

Goal

To improve the Foxcore situation, taking into consideration time and budget constraints, a database system to digitize the business operational processes accounting for sales, product, event, and salesperson will be designed and developed.

The objective of this is to

  1. Reduce data inconsistency
  2. Enhance decision-making ahead of the event in 30 days
  3. Developing the database will involve defining the relationship between the entities so that the system would effectively track events, sales, products and activities of sales consultants. Also adding security for the data collected and utilizing access to data from multiple sources

Action

The dataset was created with the following tables:

  • Booth
  • Shift
  • EventType
  • Employee
  • EventVenue
  • Product
  • Sales
  • SalesPerson

    • Conclusion

    Used the database to find answers to the following questions

    1. Calculate commission for Salespersons who participated in a particular event. Assuming a 10% commission rate.
    2. Find which Salesperson worked at a particular event in what booth and at what shift time
    3. Finding stock quantity for each product
    4. Track the product sold at each event
